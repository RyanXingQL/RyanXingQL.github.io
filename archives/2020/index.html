I’m a first-year PhD student at MC2 Lab, Beihang University, co-advised by Professor Mai Xu and Zhenyu Guan. I received the B.S. degree from Shenyuan Honors College and the School of Electronic and Information Engineering, Beihang University in 2019. My research interests mainly include image/video processing, video coding and computer vision. I am working on solving the challenge of visual data explosion.

我是北京航空航天大学MC2实验室的一年级博士生，师从徐迈教授和关振宇教授。我于2019年取得了北航荣誉教育学位和电子信息工程学士学位。我的研究领域主要包括可视化数据（图像和视频）处理和视频编码等计算机视觉任务。我致力于解决可视化数据爆炸式增长所带来的挑战。
