CV

I’m a PhD student at MC2 Lab, Beihang University, co-advised by Professor Mai Xu and Zhenyu Guan. I received my B.E. degree from Shenyuan Honors College and the School of Electronic and Information Engineering, Beihang University in 2019. My research interests include image/video enhancement, video coding and other low-level vision tasks. I am working on solving the challenge of visual data explosion.

我是北京航空航天大学MC2实验室博士生，师从徐迈和关振宇教授。我于2019年取得了北航荣誉教育学位和电子信息工程学士学位。我的研究领域包括图像/视频增强和视频编码。我致力于解决可视化数据爆炸式增长所带来的挑战。
